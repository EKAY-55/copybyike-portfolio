    About

    Psychology first.
    Copy second.

    My name is Ike. I'm a SaaS and tech copywriter based in Abuja, Nigeria, building a focused career in conversion-driven writing for tech and software brands.

    I believe the best copy doesn't feel like copy. It feels like someone finally understood your problem before offering you a solution. That's the standard I write to.

    Every piece I write starts with one question: what does this person actually fear, and what do they actually want? The words come after that answer is clear.

    My background in accounting gives me a natural instinct for structure, precision and understanding how business decisions are made — which shows up directly in how I approach enterprise and B2B copy.

    Research before writing

    Every piece starts with a customer avatar — a deep understanding of the reader's fears, desires and decision-making process.

    Conversion-focused structure

    I use proven frameworks — problem, agitation, solution — to move readers from awareness to action without pressure.

    Voice that sounds human

    No corporate speak. No empty buzzwords. Copy that sounds like a person talking to another person — specific, direct and real.

    SaaS and tech niche

    Specialized in fintech, productivity, communication and enterprise marketing — industries where precise copy moves real revenue.
