    About me

    Psychology first.
    Copy second.

    My name is Ike. I'm a Junior Accountant at the Rural Electrification Agency in Abuja, Nigeria — and I'm building a serious career in SaaS and tech copywriting.

    I believe the best copy doesn't feel like copy. It feels like someone finally understood your problem before offering you a solution. That's the standard I write to.

    Every piece I write starts with one question: what does this person actually fear, and what do they actually want? The copy comes after that answer.

    🧠

    Psychology-driven research

    Every piece starts with a customer avatar — a deep understanding of the reader's fears, desires and decision making process.

    🎯

    Conversion-focused structure

    I use proven frameworks — problem, agitation, solution — to build copy that moves readers from awareness to action.

    ✍️

    Voice that sounds human

    No corporate speak. No empty buzzwords. Copy that sounds like a person talking to another person — specific, direct and real.

    📈

    SaaS and tech niche focus

    Specialized in fintech, productivity, communication and enterprise marketing — industries where clear copy moves real revenue.
